BEING PREPARED IS VITAL AT EVERY LEVEL

Red Cross has been supporting people and communities through disasters for more than a century. And, if there is one thing we've learned, it's the importance of planning and preparedness. By understanding the risks associated with natural disasters, we can better protect ourselves, our families, and our communities.

A string of natural disasters has left many Australians feeling overwhelmed. The increased frequency and severity of these events have made recovery more difficult. Recovery doesn't happen overnight and is a long term journey that could take years
